Introduction

Welcome to SprayMix! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Name and Phone Number – Required for account creation and user identification.
  • Location Data – Used to retrieve real-time weather conditions (temperature and wind speed) for your area.
  • User Input Data – Optional details such as wheat type, nozzle type, and tank size for an enhanced experience.

How We Use Your Data

  • Providing accurate weather information based on your location.
  • Allowing users to draw a polygon on the map for area calculations.
  • Enhancing the user experience by storing relevant input data.

Third-Party Services

We use a third-party Weather API to fetch temperature and wind speed for your location. No personal data is shared with third parties.

Data Protection & Security

  • We only store your name and phone number securely.
  • Your location data is not stored; it is only used in real-time to fetch weather information.
  • We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

Data Deletion & User Rights

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time within the app.
  • Contact us for any concerns about your data privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
